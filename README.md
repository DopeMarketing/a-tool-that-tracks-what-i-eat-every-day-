# A tool that tracks what I eat every day. 

## Overview
A calorie tracking app that allows users to log food through photos or manual input and provides real-time feedback on diet adherence. The app uses image recognition to identify food items and calculate nutritional content automatically.

## Problem
Manual calorie counting is time-consuming and error-prone, making it difficult for people to consistently track their food intake and stay accountable to their dietary goals

## Target User
Health-conscious individuals, dieters, and fitness enthusiasts who want to monitor their caloric intake and maintain dietary goals

## Features
- Photo-based food recognition and calorie estimation
- Manual food entry with searchable database
- Daily calorie goal setting and tracking
- Diet adherence alerts and notifications
- Basic nutritional breakdown (calories, macros)
